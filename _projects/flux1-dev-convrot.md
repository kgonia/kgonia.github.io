ComfyUI-ready ConvRot quantizations of FLUX.1-dev. The release includes W8A8
and W4A4 diffusion-model variants, plus an optional INT8 ConvRot T5-XXL text
encoder. Use ComfyUI's core `UNETLoader`; no custom loader is required.
